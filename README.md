# Trader Behavior vs Market Sentiment Analysis

## Objective
Analyze how Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear/Greed) impacts trader behavior and performance on Hyperliquid.

## Methodology
- Cleaned and merged sentiment and trading datasets
- Analyzed performance metrics (PnL, win rate)
- Studied behavior patterns (trade size, frequency, long/short ratio)
- Visualized results using charts

## Key Insights
- Traders perform best during Extreme Greed conditions
- Fear leads to overtrading and higher risk exposure
- No strong directional bias observed across sentiment regimes

## Strategy Recommendations
- Reduce risk and avoid overtrading during Fear periods
- Use momentum-based strategies during Greed conditions
